Animation Direction — Hero Section

Which vibe fits the SME Empowerment Program best? All options keep performance solid with Framer Motion.

A — Smooth & Elegant

Staggered fade-up entrances, soft glow pulses on hover, gentle floating particles in the background, spring-physics buttons. Polished and professional — feels trustworthy. Best for a global SME audience that spans tech and non-tech users.

B — Bold & Dynamic

Typewriter headline with cursor blink, animated number counters on the trust strip, parallax depth layers on scroll, magnetic button spring bounce on hover. High energy — feels modern and innovative. Great for signaling "cutting-edge AI platform."

C — Tech & Data-Driven

Subtle grid/dot-pattern background animation, glowing metric counters, word-by-word headline reveal (not typewriter), animated tag chips for key benefits. Feels technical and data-forward. Works well if the audience skews toward founders who are already AI-literate.
