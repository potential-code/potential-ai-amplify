Which pre-prompt cards feel most useful?

Same layout, different card content. Pick the set that best matches what users actually need on day one.

1

Business-goal focused

Cards that map to concrete business outcomes — plan, grow, pitch, learn.

2

Resource-discovery focused

Cards that help users find what's on the platform — courses, mentors, events, offers.

3

Mixed (goal + discovery)

Two goal-oriented cards + two discovery cards. Best of both.
